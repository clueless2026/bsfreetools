Getitdone is a privacy-first Progressive Web App (PWA) specifically designed for the bsfreetools.com ecosystem. It provides high-velocity task tracking with manual bulk-deletion controls, ensuring the user remains in complete command of their data. By storing all mission-critical deadlines locally, Getitdone eliminates the risk of external data tracking while helping users maintain a high momentum score through consistent, visible progress tracking and overdue alerts.
